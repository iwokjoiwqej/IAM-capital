Strategy Architecture

A practical framework for running crypto capital with hard limits and clear reporting.

Core Mandate

What We Actually Run

We run three engines: Yield, Systematic Trading, and Strategic Investing. The objective is steady compounding with controlled downside.

  • M Capital preservation comes before return targeting
  • M Rule-based execution over discretionary impulse
  • M No guaranteed IRR at any stage
Scope

In Scope / Out of Scope

In scope: conservative yield structures, drawdown-controlled systematic trading, and thesis-led strategic positions.

  • U Out of scope: high-leverage discretionary trading
  • U Out of scope: low-liquidity, weakly-vetted protocol exposure
  • U Out of scope: cross-exchange arbitrage in this phase
Framework
