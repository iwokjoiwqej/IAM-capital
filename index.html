Risk Framework

Clear guardrails at the firm and strategy level. Automation is monitored, logged, and fail-safe by design.

  • ✓ Portfolio drawdown cap (e.g., 2%–5% threshold)

  • ✓ Max drawdown per strategy + auto-disable
  • ✓ Position sizing rules & leverage caps
