Risk Framework

Clear guardrails at the firm and strategy level. Automation is monitored, logged, and fail-safe by design.

Firm-level controls

  • ✓ Portfolio drawdown cap (e.g., 2%–5% threshold)
  • ✓ Kill-switch: halt all strategies and review
  • ✓ Exposure limits by asset, venue, and correlation regime

Strategy-level controls

  • ✓ Max drawdown per strategy + auto-disable
  • ✓ Position sizing rules & leverage caps
  • ✓ Trade frequency limits & cooldown windows

Operational controls

  • ✓ Real-time monitoring, logs, and alerting
  • ✓ Manual override procedures
  • ✓ Fee-aware execution (maker-first where possible)
